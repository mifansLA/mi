## Deskripsi Google Camera

Never miss a moment with Google Camera's latest speed improvement, and take fantastic pictures with new features such as auto-HDR+.

![Google Camera APK Terbaru](https://cdn57.androidauthority.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/Google-Camera-interface-Nexus-6P-Android-N.jpg)

### Features

1. **Fast switching**, Switch quickly between video and photo mode with a quick swipe.
2. **Easy access to photo modes**, Easily create stunning photographic effects and switch between photo modes (such as Lens Blur, Wide Angle, Panorama, and Photo Sphere) from the top-left menu. 
3. **Auto-HDR+**, Google Camera automatically detects when HDR+ (_High Dynamic Range + Low Light_) should be used to capture great photos in low light and backlit scenes on the Nexus 6, Nexus 5X, and Nexus 6p
4. **Slow motion video**, Record slow motion videos in 120fps on the Nexus 5X and up to 240fps on the Nexus 6P.
5. **SmartBurst**, Hold down the shutter button on the Nexus 6P to automatically capture a stream of photos and automatically make GIFs.

### Requirements

- Google Camera works on Nexus phones running Android 6.0 and above
- SmartBurst is only available on the Nexus 6P
- Slow motion video is only available on the Nexus 5X and 6P
- Photo Sphere and Panorama require a gyro sensor
- Photo Sphere, Panorama, and Lens Blur require at least 1 gigabyte of memory

## Memasang G-Cam

Untuk instalasi Google Camera APK lakukan-langkah berikut:

- Download apk gcam diatas dan letakkan di sdcard/internal storage
- Buka folder download dan buka file apk.
- Pilih install dan allow for now agar aplikasi terpasang.
- selesai.

Sedangkan (khusus) untuk **Lollipop G-Cam 3.0 portingan** harus install via recovery (TWRP). Saya coba pasang tanpa TWRP, langsung dengan ekstrak zip dulu dan ambil file di dalam folder hasil ekstrak `/system/app/GoogleCamera/GoogleCamera.apk`, hasilnya _force closed_.

Untuk pemakaiannya saat _low-light_ silahkan aktifkan HDR mode untuk hasil yang oke dan lebih jernih. Kamu juga bisa melakukan editing dengan aplikasi **Snapseed** atau **Lightroom CC** untuk hasil yang lebih baik lagi.
